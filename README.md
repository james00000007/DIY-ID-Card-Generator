# Memdeklaro de identeco - Esperanto self declaration of identity

This repo contains the informational website, privacy friendly generator, template, short ebook and 5 minute presentation

_"Self declaration of identity gives people power over their own fate, and creates a world where actions and beliefs matter more than arbitrary circumstances of birth."_

Memdeklaro offers:
- Freedom of name, freedom of belief, freedom of association
- Based on Esperanto, an anational language of peace
- Self-declared and self-printed - no database, third parties or gatekeepers

Memdeklaro is in the public domain
